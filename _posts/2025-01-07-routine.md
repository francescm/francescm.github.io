# Day to day routine

## zellij

* open a pane down: `CTRL-g p d`
* open a floating pane: `CTRL-g p w`

## lazy-vim

* open the Neotree: ``ESC :Neotree``
* move between Neotree and buffers: `LEADER w [hjkl]`
* move between buffers: `LEADER b [...]` where `[...]` can be a number or `b`
